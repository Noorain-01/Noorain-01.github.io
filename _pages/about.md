I am a PhD Candidate in the School of Computing at Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia, under the supervision of Distinguished Professor Dr. Michael Sheng and Dr. Adnan Mahmood. My research focuses on Federated Learning, Trustworthy AI, and Fair Machine Learning. I am particularly interested in developing efficient and trustworthy distributed learning algorithms that address challenges such as client heterogeneity, fairness, concept drift, and representation learning. My current research investigates fairness-aware and adaptive federated learning methods for heterogeneous environments. Before joining Macquarie University, I completed my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Electronic Engineering from Mehran University of Engineering and Technology, Pakistan.
